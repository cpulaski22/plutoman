About Plutoman

Origin Story

Maxwell Goldberg was a brilliant economist who gained supernatural powers after being struck by lightning while holding a briefcase full of cryptocurrency hardware wallets. The incident left him with plutomanita - an insatiable obsession with wealth accumulation that now drives his every action.

Mission

Plutoman uses his unique abilities to fight financial crime, expose corrupt corporations, and redistribute wealth throughout the city. However, his heroic nature is complicated by his compulsive need to always keep a substantial finder's fee for his services.

Base of Operations

The Gold Fortress serves as Plutoman's headquarters - a massive underground vault system beneath Wall Street, filled with his ever-growing collection of assets, investments, and emergency cryptocurrency reserves.

Powers & Abilities

Money Magnetism

Plutoman can attract and manipulate any form of currency, precious metals, or valuable assets within a 50-mile radius. This power is often used to "liberate" dirty money from criminal organizations.

Market Precognition

He possesses the ability to see into the financial future, predicting stock movements, cryptocurrency crashes, and economic trends with 99.7% accuracy. This information is never shared freely.

Compound Interest Strength

His physical strength grows exponentially based on his net worth. Currently capable of bench-pressing 2.5 tons and lifting a fully armored bank truck with ease.

Digital Transformation

Plutoman can transform his body into pure financial energy, allowing him to travel through banking networks, ATMs, and digital payment systems instantaneously.
